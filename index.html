Southwestern University of Finance and Economics

Fall 2021

Course Overview

This course is the same as FEG521: Financial Stochastic Analysis this semester. The goal of these two courses are to provide an introduction to the application of stochastic in finance to the third year students major in financial engineering and fintech. In this course, you will learn: (a) Discrete-time finance, such as single-period market model (b) Continuous-time finance, such as Black-Scholes model.
